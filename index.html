Overview

From coordinator agents to Kanban cards.

I specialize in Generative AI — multi-agent orchestration with LangGraph, retrieval-augmented generation over FAISS and vector embeddings, and prompt systems that hold up outside a demo. My multi-agent research assistant, Argus, runs a critic agent purely to catch its own hallucinations before they reach the user.

I don't stop at the model. FitVibe, AutoAgent, and AuraMatch are full products: Flask/FastAPI backends, guardrail validators, thread-safe state stores, and React or Next.js front ends deployed and reachable right now, not just described in a README.

Outside of code, I'm minoring in Economics — I've written on public policy for Rishihood's Public Policy Club and co-authored research on the Phillips curve and AI-driven labor shifts. I'd rather build the thing and understand the system it lives in than do just one.
